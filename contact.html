About Barbara Morrison

About

Barbara Morrison

I am a Junior Data Analyst with a bioengineering degree. I have experience teaching Middle School, High School and Junior College, as well as working as a quality auditor in an electronics manufacturing background.

My abilities and interests lend well to a data analyst job. By nature, I am a very detail-oriented and analytical person. Consequently, I enjoy data analysis work.

I have seven years of experience creating visuals for college students to help them understand complex scientific and mathematical concepts. Throughout my education career, I’ve seen the immense value of effective visualizations, which prompted me to pursue further education to refine my skills in data collection, cleaning, and manipulation. Some of my projects include:
• Staffing Distribution: Strategically assigned key medical staff ahead of the flu season using Excel, VLOOKUP, Python, and Tableau for data visualization.
• Ecommerce Buying Patterns: Examined data to identify the correlation between customer buying behavior and ecommerce marketing strategy using NumPy, pandas, Jupyter, matplotlib, and Excel.
• Market Research: Conducted analysis to identify opportunities for a movie rental shop’s transition to an online service using Tableau, Excel, and SQL queries.

Specialties: data analysis, Microsoft Excel, data cleaning, statistics, data visualization, requirements gathering, project management, SQL, relational databases.
